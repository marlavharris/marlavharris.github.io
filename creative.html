03 — BAO BAO ISSEY MIYAKE

Luxury E-Commerce Strategy

Transforming innovative geometric design into a seamless digital experience. BAO BAO ISSEY MIYAKE creates bags that shift from flat planes to 3D forms, bringing beauty, wonder, and utility to daily life.

The Challenge

Translating the tactile, shape-shifting nature of the product into a high-converting digital storefront while overcoming legacy platform limitations.

The Strategy

Full-Site Re-platform & Data-Led Merchandising. Spearheaded the migration from Magento to Shopify, led QA testing, and implemented a disciplined A/B testing program to optimize UX. Managed the integrated marketing calendar and directed seasonal photoshoots to align creative with commercial goals.

The Result

Drove a 15% Increase in Conversion Rate & Reduced Cost-Per-Conversion by 30%.
