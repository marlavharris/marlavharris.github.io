// The Story

From Bra Fitter to Data Leader

Former bra fitter with a psychic‑like ability to predict trends. Just kidding, it’s all in the data.

I’m an ex–fashion design student turned luxury marketer obsessed with making everything look more expensive—even a $5.99 subscription. The philosophy is simple: "If I can sell a $300 bra, I can sell anything."

For 8+ years, I’ve worked at the intersection of product, marketing, and technology, turning messy spreadsheets and marketing tech into strategies that drive 17% YoY revenue growth.

Now? I’m pivoting. I went back to school for my Master’s in Public Administration to combine analytics, storytelling, and marketing in ways that strengthen the relationship between audiences and businesses.

"Making money and taking care of communities aren’t contradictions. The competition isn’t my colleagues—it’s creating engaging content that sells."
